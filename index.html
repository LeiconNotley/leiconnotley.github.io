50+Years of Heritage

From Notley & Co to a Leading Multi-Discipline Contractor

Leicon Notley has been delivering a variety of projects for over 40 years having developed from the proud piping and fabrication heritage of Notley and Co. (1971) to the hands-on, design and construct, multi-discipline engineering contractor we are today.

We offer complete end-to-end solutions for multi-discipline projects from concept design through to commissioning and handover. In recent years, we have developed specific expertise in all aspects of water and wastewater collection, treatment, management and distribution.

With the inclusion of Leicon Electrical and Controls Engineering (LEC) — our electrical, switchboard manufacturing and controls subsidiary — we are uniquely positioned to deliver fully integrated, in-house solutions across a wide range of infrastructure and industrial projects.

Capabilities & Services

Comprehensive, in-house multi-discipline engineering and construction capabilities, from concept design through to commissioning and handover.

Project & Construction Management

End-to-end Head Contractor management from brownfield upgrades to new plant builds. Leicon Notley takes full ownership of project delivery as your dedicated Head Contractor, with a single point of contact and complete oversight of resources, scope, budget and schedule. Our experienced Site Supervisors manage day-to-day site activities, subcontractor coordination and HSEQ compliance, all underpinned by our proprietary ISO-certified Leicon Management System (LMS).

Civil & Structural

Structural erection and concrete works to trenching and site services, Leicon Notley provides the construction capability to bring your project to life. As a Registered Builder (BC 12995), we deliver compliant building works backed by certified confined space and working-at-heights teams. Our Telstra-accredited Dial Before You Dig (DBYD) cable locator services add an extra layer of safety and assurance — protecting your assets, your people, and your project timeline.

Mechanical & Piping

Process piping to pumps, blowers, and tanks — Leicon Notley delivers mechanical installations built to last. Our welders and fitters work to AS 4041 and ASME IX standards, ensuring every joint, valve, and component meets the highest level of quality and compliance. Whether your project calls for plastic, carbon, stainless, or MSCL pipework, our experienced team has the capability to design, fabricate, and install with precision.

Fabrication Workshop

With over 8,000 m² of workshop and yard space, Leicon Notley has the capacity, capability, and craftsmanship to deliver fabrication projects of any size. From process piping and structural steel to stainless, aluminium, and plastic welding. We also design and build modular process plants, including membrane filtration, RO and chemical dosing skids, pump skids, and complete prefabricated water treatment facilities — delivered in sea containers or steel-framed buildings.

Borefields, Pipelines & Pumping

Water Infrastructure Delivered End-to-End from borefields and pump stations to pipelines and complete water supply networks — Leicon Notley delivers the infrastructure that keeps Western Australia flowing. With decades of bore fitting and upgrade experience across the State, our team handles every stage from installation through to testing, commissioning, documentation, and handover — ensuring your system is delivered ready to perform from day one.

Water & Wastewater Treatment

Leicon Notley delivers specialist expertise in all aspects of water and wastewater collection, treatment, management and distribution. Our team designs and builds membrane plants, chemical dosing skids, and packaged process systems tailored to each client's needs. We're particularly proud of our brownfield upgrade capability — with a proven track record of managing complex shutdown interfaces to keep your operations running while we deliver the upgrades you need.

Switchboard Manufacturing

Our in-house electrical subsidiary LEC, is an accredited CUBIC Switchboard Manufacturer and the holder of Electrical Contractor's Licence EC009399, we design and build AS 61439 Design Verified assemblies that meet the highest standards of safety, performance, and compliance. With LEC as part of the Leicon Notley family, our clients benefit from seamless integration between mechanical, civil, and electrical disciplines — all engineered, manufactured, and delivered in-house.

Electrical, Instrumentation & Controls

Leicon Notley delivers the intelligence that brings plants to life. Through our in-house subsidiary LEC, our team programs and configures across all major platforms — Siemens, Schneider, Allen-Bradley, GE, and Koyo — and builds powerful HMI and SCADA systems using GeoSCADA and CitectSCADA. Whether your project runs on Ethernet, Profinet, Modbus, Fieldbus, or Profibus, we design, integrate, and commission control systems that connect, communicate, and perform.

Leicon Electrical & Controls Engineering (LEC)

LEC is our specialist electrical and controls subsidiary — delivering design, switchboard manufacturing, SCADA works and electrical installation.


Engineered Switchboard Solutions — Designed, Assembled & Commissioned in WA

We specialise in the end-to-end delivery of electrical switchboard assemblies — from initial concept design through to manufacture, installation, and commissioning. Every switchboard we produce is AS/NZS 61439 Design Verified, ensuring full compliance with Australian standards and giving our clients confidence in safety, reliability, and performance.

What Sets Us Apart

As an accredited CUBIC Switchboard Manufacturer, we combine industry-leading modular platform technology with hands-on craftsmanship. Our in-house team delivers detailed switchboard and control panel designs purpose-built for manufacturing and assembly — eliminating guesswork and ensuring seamless integration on site.

Every unit undergoes rigorous Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) at our dedicated manufacturing facility in Perth, Western Australia, so it arrives ready to perform from day one.
