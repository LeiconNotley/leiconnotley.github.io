AS/NZS 61439Design Verified

Powering industry with reliable switchboards and control systems

Leicon Electrical & Controls Engineering (LEC) is a Western Australian-based provider of specialised electrical engineering, switchboard manufacturing, and industrial control solutions.

We specialise in the design and manufacture of compliant, high-performance electrical switchboards, integrated control systems, and automation solutions across the water, wastewater, infrastructure, and industrial sectors.

As an accredited CUBIC Switchboard Manufacturer, LEC combines industry-leading modular platform technology with hands-on craftsmanship. Every switchboard we produce is AS/NZS 61439 Design Verified, ensuring full compliance with Australian standards.

A subsidiary of Leicon Pty Ltd, LEC combines the backing of an established engineering and construction company with specialist electrical, instrumentation and controls expertise — delivering integrated solutions from design through to commissioning.

LEC is proudly West Australian owned and operated.

Capabilities & Services

Comprehensive, in-house multi-discipline engineering and construction capabilities, from concept design through to commissioning and handover.

Engineering & Design

From engineering studies and conceptual design through to detailed design and technical specifications, LEC delivers specialist engineering and design services across the resources, power and water sectors. Our in-house team covers the full project lifecycle, including equipment selection, technical specifications, Arc Flash Hazard Assessments and Protection Design, Earthing Design and Studies, and Soil Resistivity Testing supporting safe, compliant and high-performing electrical infrastructure.

Switchboard Design & Manufacture

LEC are specialist in design, manufacture, installation and commissioning of electrical switchboards. All assemblies are delivered as AS/NZS 61439 Design Verified switchboards, ensuring compliance, performance and reliability across all projects. Products include Modular Switchboard Systems, Switchrooms, MCCs, Distribution Boards, Marshalling Panels, Solar Arrays, Remote IO, and Control & SCADA Cubicles.

Electrical Installation

LEC has multi-skilled field teams delivering installation of electrical infrastructure across high and low voltage systems. Expertise includes switchgear, motor control centres, starters, motors and control equipment. Underground cabling systems — power, control, data and telemetry. Modifications and upgrading of electrical equipment. Cable terminations including high voltage.

Process Instrumentation

LEC supplies, installs, calibrates and commissions process measurement instrumentation across water infrastructure and industrial projects — including control valves, level sensors, flow meters and all field instrument requirements. Calibrated and verified prior to handover, every system delivers accurate, reliable measurement and seamless integration with control and SCADA platforms.

Control, SCADA & Automation

Control systems, engineered end-to-end. From concept through to commissioning and handover, LEC delivers integrated control solutions for water infrastructure and industrial projects. We analyse process requirements, integrate the right hardware and software, and design secure, resilient communication networks, all backed by detailed specifications and rigorous Factory Acceptance Testing. The result is lower risk, faster commissioning, proven performance from day one.

Communications & Integration

LEC designs and delivers complete communications and integration solutions — from field instruments through to enterprise-level platforms. Our team covers communications design, site surveys and desktop studies, plus the programming and configuration of Ethernet, PROFINET, PROFIBUS, Modbus, Fieldbus, radio and fibre networks. The result: seamless data flow, total system interoperability and reliable performance from sensor to SCADA.

Engineered Switchboard Solutions — Designed, Assembled & Commissioned in WA

We specialise in the end-to-end delivery of electrical switchboard assemblies — from initial design through to manufacture, installation, and commissioning. Every switchboard we produce is AS/NZS 61439 Design Verified, ensuring full compliance with Australian standards and giving our clients confidence in safety, reliability, and performance.

What Sets Us Apart

As an accredited CUBIC Switchboard Manufacturer, we combine industry-leading modular platform technology with hands-on craftsmanship. Our in-house team delivers detailed switchboard and control panel designs purpose-built for manufacturing and assembly — eliminating guesswork and ensuring seamless integration on site.

Every unit undergoes rigorous Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) at our dedicated manufacturing facility in Perth, Western Australia, so it arrives ready to perform from day one.
